BIOGRAPHY

Oyetunde, Metomi Samuel is a graduate of Theatre and Media Arts, Ekiti State University, Ado Ekiti. He hails from Ekiti State, and has lived most of his life there. He majored in Public Relations, and has chosen the study of interpersonal relationships as a personal career. He has achieved this through his undiluted dedication and focus on both intensive and extensive studies which he does through various online researches, books and some other beneficial materials. His principal aim is becoming an eminent public relations consultant. He got his first degree in the year (2019), and he went for NYSC afterwards. All through his stay at campus, he underwent a fashion design training which he later placed on hold after garnering the knowledge enough for his personal use. After his NYSC, he found his way into graphic designs and printing, which he learnt at Easy Bee Global Company Inc between 2021 & 2022. On his journey to furthering his education, Tech found him, which placed his education furtherance on hold. He found his way into the Tech Industry, and that gave a boost and edge to his career as a Product Designer and Backend Developer. As it is now, Metomi is passionately motivated to achieve his goal in the tech industry, which is to touch the world with his name. read more...
